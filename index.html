Prototype

ESP32 Wokwi Project

Interactive simulation: ESP32 DevKit C v4, HX711 load cell, HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor, and a red alert LED.

Open Wokwi project
Sketch

Current firmware behavior

  • Reads load-cell weight from the HX711 on GPIO 21/22.
  • Measures water depth with the ultrasonic sensor on GPIO 5/18.
  • Turns the red LED on GPIO 15 on when trash weight exceeds 1000g and water level is at or above 90%.
